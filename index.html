That's not entirely true!

Requirement 1

Needs to be business driven

  • Data used to solve real business problems

  • Save money

  • Make Money

  • Identify new markets & products

Anti-Pattens

Your boss...

  • just read an article on Machine Learning

  • heard "data is the new oil"

  • went to a conference with "tech" in title

Requirement 2

Strong Corporate Sponsor

  • Project has to be lead by senior board member

  • CEO / COO - operational role preferable

  • They will provide cover & run interference

  • You need an open & honest relationship with them

Anti-Pattens

Beware!

  • CIO lead - tends to be too tech focused

  • CDO lead - data governance & compliance

  • CFO lead - justifing ROI hard

Requirement 3

Start Small

  • Agile, small, focused, good communicators

  • 2 - 6 high quality people

  • pragmatic, generalists, creative

  • deep business domain knowledge essential

  • These people are hard to find - pay them well

Anti-Pattens

This actually happens

  • I need to hire 50 data scientists now!

  • Wait for a year - you have spent $1,000,000

  • I call this "The Walking Dead"

  • Initally hire weak or substandard - get what you pay for

Requirement 4

Use the best tools - FOSS

  • Still an issue with companies

  • The ability to easily try new tools is important

  • Fight hard for this

  • Write a "Manifesto" - fait accompli

  • Corollary - Benovelent IT

Anti-Pattens

We've all been here

  • Locked down Windows PC

  • Can't install any software

  • takes weeks for IT to do anything

  • Get out now - while you still can

Requirement 5

Encourage creativity & embrace failure

  • Data Science is just that - a science

  • It's highly experimental - fail often, fail fast

  • The best ideas come at weird times

  • Some of the best work we've done was by accident

  • This is really hard for companies - blame management

Anti-Pattens

  • Innovation theatre

  • BAU - dressed up as R & D

  • Flogging a dead horse

  • No mechanism for admitting failure

Conclusion

  • Needs to be business driven

  • Strong Corporate Sponsor

  • Start Small

  • Use the best tools - FOSS

  • Allow for & embrace failure
